What Makes Hoang Special?

Experienced teaching staff with more than 15 years of teaching experience in the profession, from all over the world

HOANG is an online interactive platform and system that allows users to learn, practice and evaluate their programming skills through practice exercises, coding contests with automatic grading function for users Use can improve skills and increase productivity.

We focus on developing a comprehensive ecosystem with beginner to advanced courses and practice challenges suitable for everyone. With a wide variety of courses and challenges that support many different programming languages, HOANG help users learn and practice programming easily and efficiently. We connect all those who share the same passion for the field of computer science, then together build a strong community
